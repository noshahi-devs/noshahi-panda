What you need to get started

Legal Registration

Your business must be officially registered. Requirements include:

  • Valid Food Authority License (PFA/similar)
  • Trade License for retail/stores
  • Registration with local municipal bodies

Essential Documents

Ensure you have the following ready to upload:

  • CNIC of the owner (Front/Back)
  • NTN (National Tax Number) Certificate
  • Copy of a Cancelled Check or Bank Statement

Menu & Pricing

Prepare your digital storefront:

  • Full list of items with descriptions
  • Clear, tax-inclusive pricing
  • Availability hours and category labels

High Quality Photos

Visuals matter for sales performance:

  • Sharp, bright images of signature dishes
  • Clean background with natural lighting
  • Cover photo of your restaurant/shopfront
